Kentucky Housing Choropleth Map

Showing Vacant Properties

Map authored by Daniel Cockayne

About This Map

This is a choropleth map showing the number of occupied properties (rented or owned) expressed as a proportion of the total properties per county in Kentucky.

Click on a county to see its name, the number of occupied and total properties, and the percentage of homes occupied in that county. In each county, the darker the blue, the higher the proportion of occupied properties in the county.
